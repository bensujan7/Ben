Cristiano Ronaldo (born February 5, 1985) is a Portuguese football forward
who is one of the greatest players of his generation. The winner
of five Ballon d’Or awards, he is among the sport’s top goal scorers.

He is the inspiration of millions people and most followed person in the planet Earth.
He has inspired the people not only in football but also in different field by his
hardwork and dedication towards the game.
